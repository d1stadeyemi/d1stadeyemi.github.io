Metagenomics Pipeline (Large-Scale Data Analysis)

Designed and implemented scalable pipelines to process over 1 TB of metagenomic sequencing data using Python, Bash, and HPC (SLURM).

Reconstructed 37 high-quality genomes and identified metabolic pathways involved in nitrogen, sulfur, and carbon cycling.

Biosynthetic Gene Cluster Discovery

Identified over 320 biosynthetic gene clusters using antiSMASH and PRISM, including novel candidates not present in existing databases.

This work contributes to the discovery of potential bioactive compounds, including antibiotic candidates.

Regulatory Genomics (TFBS & Network Analysis)

Performed promoter sequence analysis and motif discovery (MEME Suite) to identify transcription factor binding patterns.

Integrated transcriptomic data to construct gene regulatory networks and identify key regulatory hubs.

RNA-seq Analysis

Conducted differential expression analysis using DESeq2 and limma to identify genes associated with biological conditions.

Single-Cell RNA-seq (Cancer Microenvironment)

Analyzed single-cell RNA-seq data to identify cell populations and characterize tumor microenvironment heterogeneity using clustering (UMAP, Leiden) and marker gene analysis.

Machine Learning in Genomics

Applied Random Forest models and feature engineering approaches to extract predictive signals from biological datasets.
