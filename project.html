Decoding Life — One Genome at a Time

Selected Projects

Metagenomics

Large-Scale Metagenomics Pipeline

Designed and implemented scalable pipelines to process over 1 TB of metagenomic sequencing data using Python, Bash, and HPC (SLURM). Reconstructed 37 high-quality metagenome-assembled genomes and identified metabolic pathways involved in nitrogen, sulfur, and carbon cycling.

Drug Discovery

Biosynthetic Gene Cluster Discovery

Identified over 320 biosynthetic gene clusters using antiSMASH and PRISM, including novel candidates absent from existing databases. This work contributes to the discovery of potential bioactive compounds, including antibiotic candidates.

Regulatory Genomics

TFBS & Network Analysis

Performed promoter sequence analysis and motif discovery (MEME Suite) to identify transcription factor binding patterns. Integrated transcriptomic data to construct gene regulatory networks and identify key regulatory hubs.

Transcriptomics

RNA-seq Differential Expression

Conducted differential expression analysis using DESeq2 and limma to identify genes associated with biological conditions and disease states.

Single-Cell

scRNA-seq: Cancer Microenvironment

Analyzed single-cell RNA-seq data to identify cell populations and characterize tumor microenvironment heterogeneity using clustering (UMAP, Leiden) and marker gene analysis.

Machine Learning

ML in Genomics

Applied Random Forest models and feature engineering approaches to extract predictive signals from biological datasets, with ongoing work in PyTorch-based sequence modeling.
